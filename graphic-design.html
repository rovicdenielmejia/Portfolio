Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with startups and SMEs?

Yes. I specialize in startups and growing SMEs in the Philippines that need clear brand systems and marketing visuals without agency pricing.

How long does a logo or brand project take?

Timelines vary by scope. Logo projects typically take 1–2 weeks. Full brand systems may take 2–4 weeks. Campaign visuals and one-off designs often deliver within days.

What file formats do I receive?

You receive print-ready (PDF, high-res PNG) and digital formats as needed. Source files and usage guidelines are included for scalable projects.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Revisions are included in the scope. The number depends on the package; we align on this before starting.
