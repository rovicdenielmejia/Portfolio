Frequently Asked Questions

What roles do you recruit for?

Operations, store-based staff, corporate & admin roles, creative & marketing positions, technical support, and entry-level to supervisory roles. Executive and specialist sourcing is available case-to-case.

How long does recruitment take?

Hiring timelines depend on role complexity and market demand. Most structured recruitment cycles range from 2–4 weeks.

Do you help startups build HR from scratch?

Yes. I work with startups and SMEs that need foundational HR documentation, employee handbooks, policies, and structured workforce processes.

Are your HR services compliant with Philippine labor practices?

Documentation is structured using best-practice standards and awareness of Philippine labor regulations. It does not replace formal legal counsel for specific legal matters.
