Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about my creative and workforce strategy services.

What services do you offer?

I provide Brand & Visual Strategy and Workforce & HR Strategy services. This includes logo and brand systems, marketing creatives, recruitment support, and structured HR documentation for growing businesses.

Do you work with startups or established companies?

Both. I support startups building systems from scratch and SMEs that need structured brand or HR foundations for scaling.

How does the recruitment process work?

It begins with role alignment and structured screening. Candidates are endorsed after evaluation, followed by interview coordination and onboarding support.

Do you offer ongoing HR support?

Yes. I provide project-based HR consulting and monthly workforce partnership (retainer) models for businesses that require continuous recruitment or HR system oversight.

What industries do you support?

Retail, corporate, technical, creative, operations, and SME environments across the Philippines.

How do we start?

You can schedule a consultation or submit an inquiry form. All engagements begin with a structured discussion of scope, goals, and timeline.
