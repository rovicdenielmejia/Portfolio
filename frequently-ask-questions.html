Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about Brand Strategy Philippines, recruitment and HR consulting in PH, workforce solutions Philippines, and engagement models, for SMEs and growing businesses.

What does a Brand Strategist in the Philippines do?

As a Brand Strategist in the Philippines, I help businesses build structured visual identity systems, including logo design, brand guidelines, marketing collaterals, and platform-ready creative assets aligned with market positioning.

Do you provide brand strategy for startups and SMEs in PH?

Yes. I support Philippine startups and growing SMEs that need clear brand positioning, consistent visual identity, and scalable design systems.

What’s included in your Brand & Visual Strategy services?

Services include logo systems, typography & color strategy, marketing layouts, event creatives, print-ready files, and digital brand assets tailored to business goals.

How is your approach different from typical graphic designers?

Unlike purely design-focused freelancers, I build brand systems aligned with business growth, audience targeting, and operational structure.

Do you offer full brand systems or logo-only services?

Both are available. However, businesses seeking long-term scalability are encouraged to invest in complete brand systems.

Do you provide Recruitment Services in the Philippines?

Yes. As a Recruitment Partner, I provide structured hiring support and workforce recruitment solutions for SMEs and scaling businesses, per-hire, project-based, and retainer models.

What roles do you recruit for in PH?

I recruit for operations, store-based staff, corporate & admin roles, creative & marketing positions, and technical support roles across Philippine-based companies.

How does your recruitment process work?

The process includes role alignment, structured talent sourcing, screening, candidate endorsement, interview coordination, and onboarding support.

How long does recruitment take in the Philippines?

Hiring timelines depend on role complexity and market demand. Most structured recruitment cycles range from 2–4 weeks.

Do you offer ongoing recruitment support?

Yes. Through the Strategic Recruitment Partner model, businesses can engage in a monthly workforce retainer for continuous recruitment oversight.

Do you offer HR Consulting Services in the Philippines?

Yes, I support businesses in the Philippines with HR consulting services, workforce structuring, HR systems for SMEs, and documentation aligned with Philippine labor standards.

What HR services do you provide?

HR services include: Employee Handbook Development, HR Policy Structuring, KPI & Performance Systems, Due Process Documentation, Onboarding & Offboarding Systems, and Employment Contracts & Templates.

Do you help startups build HR systems from scratch?

Yes. I work with startups and SMEs that need foundational HR documentation and structured workforce processes.

Are your HR services compliant with Philippine labor practices?

HR documentation is structured using best-practice standards and awareness of Philippine labor regulations. However, it does not replace formal legal counsel.

What’s the difference between Recruitment Services and HR Consulting?

Recruitment focuses on talent acquisition. HR consulting focuses on workforce systems, compliance structure, documentation, and performance management frameworks.

How much do recruitment services cost in the Philippines?

Pricing depends on engagement model, per-hire, project-based recruitment, or monthly workforce retainer. Scope and fees are finalized before engagement.

How much does HR consulting cost in PH?

HR consulting fees depend on documentation scope, workforce size, and engagement duration. Custom proposals are provided after consultation.

Do you offer retainer packages for recruitment and HR?

Yes. Businesses can engage in monthly workforce retainers for structured hiring support and HR advisory.

How do we start working together?

All engagements begin with a structured consultation to clarify hiring needs, HR requirements, and scope alignment.

Do you work with clients outside Metro Manila?

Yes. Recruitment and HR consulting services are provided remotely across the Philippines.
